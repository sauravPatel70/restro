Why Round the Clock Bar ?

The Round the Clock Bar is independently-owned boutique located in the Uttarakhand. We specialize in craft and artisanal spirits, as well as bitters, wines,beer, glassware and barware. We strive to support small, independently owned producers who are honest about their ingredients and put a level of care and attention into their process that can't be matched on an industrial scale. We are dedicated to amking our space friendly, accessible and approachable to both novices and seasoned connoisseurs alike.
